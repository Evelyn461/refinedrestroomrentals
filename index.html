Frequently Asked Questions

How many porta-potties do I need for my event?

We calculate this based on attendance, event duration, and the presence of food or beverages.

Can I rent porta potties for a long-term project?

Yes. We support long-term construction, municipal projects, and maintenance work with scalable servicing plans and scheduled pumping.

How soon should I book my restrooms?

Booking early is best for large events, while construction and long-term projects can be scheduled as needed.

Can I rent trailers for multi-day events?

Yes. We prepare restroom trailers for extended use with restocking and scheduled servicing.

Do you handle placement and servicing?

Yes. Our team handles placement, pumping, sanitizing, and scheduled servicing.

How often do you service the units?

Servicing frequency depends on usage volume and rental duration, and our technicians create a tailored schedule for every customer.
